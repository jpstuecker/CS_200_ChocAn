Glossary

  • Acme Accounting Services -- a third party organization responsible for financial procedures such as recording payments of membership fees, suspending members whose fees are overdue, and reinstating suspended members who have now paid what is owing

  • Chocoholics Anonymous (ChocAn) -- an organization dedicated to helping people addicted to chocolate

  • EFT Report -- a record consisting of electronic funds transfer data

  • Interactive Mode -- the way in which the ChocAn Data Center is run during the day, allowing operators to update member status and records as well as provider status and records

  • Main Accounting Procedure -- reads the week's file of services provided and prints a number of reports

  • Manager -- a ChocAn employee who is responsible for overseeing summary reports and billing information

  • Member -- an individual who pays ChocAn to receive services from providers

  • Member Number -- a nine-digit number unique to each member

  • Member Report -- a report submitted to each member detailing the services provided to that member, sorted in the order of service date

  • Operator -- A ChocAn employee that oversees member and provider records

  • Provider -- a healthcare professional who provides services to ChocAn members

  • Provider Directory -- an alphabetically ordered list of service names and corresponding service codes and fees

  • Provider Report -- a report submitted to each provider at the end of the week containing the list of services he or she provided to ChocAn members

  • Terminal -- the apparatus by which the ChocAn system is accessed by the provider

  • Service Report -- a report written to disk after each time a service is used; includes current date & time, date service was provided, provider number, member number, service code, and comments (100 characters, optional)

  • Summary Report -- a report submitted to managers listing every provider to be paid for a certain week, the number of conultations each had, and his or her total fee for that week.
